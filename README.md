## What is Document Scanner PDF?

**Document Scanner PDF** is a focused, no-frills mobile scanner for Android and iPhone. Point the camera at a document, let the app detect the edges automatically, fix the perspective, enhance the page, and export a multi-page PDF. The whole flow is on-device — no account, no upload, no subscription.

It is designed for the things people actually scan: receipts, invoices, contracts, ID cards, school assignments, business cards, handwritten notes, lecture handouts, books, and forms that need to be sent over email or uploaded to a portal.

## Key Features

### Capture
- **Camera scan** with auto-edge detection
- **Import from photos** — turn an existing photo into a scanned page
- **Multi-page capture** — keep adding pages until you're done

### Cleanup
- **Automatic perspective correction** — the page comes out flat and rectangular even if you photographed it at an angle
- **Manual edge adjustment** when auto-detection isn't quite right
- **Document filters** — clean B&W, grayscale, color, magic clean
- **Brightness and contrast** sliders for fine-tuning

### Organize
- **Multi-page documents** — add pages, delete pages, reorder pages by dragging
- **Local library** — your scans stay on the device for quick access

### Export
- **PDF export** with quality options (small / standard / high)
- **Share** to email, messaging apps, cloud drives — anything that accepts a PDF
- **Save** to your device or to your preferred cloud folder

## How Document Scanner PDF Works

**Q: How is the edge detection done?**
A: The app analyzes each frame from the camera and finds the rectangle that looks most like a document. You can tweak the corners manually if the auto-detection picks the wrong edges.

**Q: What does "magic clean" do?**
A: It boosts contrast, drops shadows, and turns smudgy paper photos into clean white pages with crisp text.

**Q: Are my scans uploaded somewhere?**
A: No. Scanning, cropping, filtering, and PDF export all happen on the device. The app does not upload your documents.

**Q: Can I make a single PDF from multiple pictures already in my gallery?**
A: Yes. Use the import option to add photos one by one, reorder them, then export the whole stack as a single PDF.

## FAQ

**Is it free?**
Yes. The app is free to download and use. No paywalled features.

**Do I need an account?**
No. There is no sign-up, no email, no login.

**Are my documents uploaded?**
No. All scanning and PDF export is done on your device.

**What's the maximum number of pages per PDF?**
There is no fixed cap. Limit is your device storage.

**Can I edit a scan after I save it?**
You can re-open a saved scan, add or remove pages, re-apply filters, and re-export.

**Why does the app need camera permission?**
Camera access is the only way to capture pages. The camera is used solely for scanning.

**Why does it need photo / media permission?**
Only to let you pick existing images to add as pages, and to save the exported PDF.

**Can it OCR (extract text from) the scan?**
This release focuses on producing a clean, shareable PDF. Searchable-text OCR is on the roadmap.

**What devices are supported?**
Any Android phone or tablet with a camera, and iPhones / iPads on iOS 13.0 or later.

**How do I report a bug?**
Open an issue at [github.com/Lapnito/document-scanner-pdf/issues](https://github.com/Lapnito/document-scanner-pdf/issues) or email tom@lapnito.cz.
